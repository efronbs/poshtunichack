Picture Telephone





This game is about seeing a word or phrase get lost in translation as words and pictures are passed around. This game has no winners and no losers.
  1. Each player draws themself.
  2. Each player thinks of something that they want another player to draw, and types in a description of it.
  3. When text gets passed to a player, the player tries to draw a picture of that thing. When a drawing gets passed to a player, the player tries to describe that thing. Players are not allowed to draw words.
  4. When each player has contributed to every drawing chain, the results are shown.
